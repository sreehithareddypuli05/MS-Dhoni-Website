Rise of a Cricket Legend

MS Dhoni’s cricket journey is nothing short of legendary. From a small-town boy to India’s greatest captain, his career is filled with iconic moments.

Dhoni's Career Timeline

2004 - International Debut

Debuted for India vs Bangladesh. Scored a duck in his first match but bounced back strongly.

2005 - First ODI Century

Scored a blazing 148 against Pakistan in Visakhapatnam, making him a household name.

2007 - T20 World Cup Winner 🏆

Led India to victory in the inaugural ICC T20 World Cup, defeating Pakistan in the final.

2011 - ODI World Cup Champion 🏆

Played a legendary 91* in the final against Sri Lanka, hitting the winning six to seal India’s World Cup win after 28 years.

2013 - ICC Champions Trophy 🏆

Led India to another major ICC trophy, defeating England in the final.

2016 - Last ICC Final

Played in the T20 World Cup semi-finals; India lost to West Indies.

2020 - International Retirement

On August 15, 2020, Dhoni announced his retirement from international cricket with an emotional video.

IPL Journey with Chennai Super Kings

MS Dhoni has been the face of **Chennai Super Kings (CSK)** since the IPL’s inception in 2008. Under his leadership, CSK has won multiple IPL titles.

  • 🏆 **2008:** Bought by CSK for $1.5 million (highest at that time).
  • 🏆 **2010, 2011, 2018, 2021, 2023:** Won **5 IPL titles** as captain.
  • 🔥 **Most successful IPL captain** in terms of consistency.

MS Dhoni's Records & Achievements

  • 🏏 **Only captain to win all ICC trophies (T20 WC, ODI WC, Champions Trophy).**
  • 💯 **Highest score by a wicketkeeper in ODIs - 183* vs Sri Lanka.**
  • 🧤 **Most stumpings in international cricket - 195 stumpings.**
  • ⚡ **Fastest stumping ever recorded - 0.08 seconds!**
  • 👑 **Most successful Indian captain in Test cricket (27 wins).**
  • 🔥 **Over 10,000 ODI runs with an average of 50+.**
