Early Life of MS Dhoni

Born: July 7, 1981
Place: Ranchi, Jharkhand (then Bihar)
Father: Pan Singh (MECON employee)
Mother: Devaki Devi (homemaker)
Siblings: Narendra Singh Dhoni (brother), Jayanti Gupta (sister)

Dhoni came from a **middle-class family** with no background in cricket. His father wanted him to focus on studies and get a stable job, but his passion for sports led him in a different direction.

Journey to Cricket Stardom

Dhoni started playing professional cricket with **Commando Cricket Club (1995-1998)** and later joined the **Bihar U-16 and U-19 teams**. However, his journey was not easy.

In 2001, to support his family, Dhoni worked as a **ticket collector (TC) at Kharagpur railway station** for nearly **3 years**. But he never gave up on his cricket dreams and continued practicing after his duty hours.

His breakthrough came in **2003**, when he was selected for the **India A team**. His performances in Kenya and Zimbabwe impressed selectors, leading to his **Team India debut in 2004**.
